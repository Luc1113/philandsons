About Us

Welcome to Phil & Sons on Main, your neighborhood destination for authentic Italian food and incredible pizza.

We specialize in oversized thin-crust slices, traditional Sicilian-style pies, and hearty Italian specialties that have been perfected over decades. Every dish is made with care and the finest ingredients.

Whether you're grabbing a slice on your lunch break or sitting down for a full meal, you'll experience the warmth and quality that define Phil & Sons.
